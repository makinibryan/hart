The Nairobi National Park Compelling Campaign!

I am a bit ashamed to say this. I was born and raised in Nairobi but had never visited Nairobi National Park, so when I heard about this specific safari I was sold!
We met at the CBD-Nairobi quite early and set off with my fellow ‘safari-mates’ (I don’t think that’s an actual word… haha!) at 6:30am. It’s about an hour drive to The Nairobi National Park.
After checking in, give or take, our safari started at 8:00am. We were all looking forward to see the different animals in the park like. You should have seen how eager we all looked looking outside the window to see the animals. No one wanted to miss the beautiful views of the park.
The first animals we saw were baboons. Lots of baboons!
The other animals we saw were giraffes, elephants, buffaloes and crocodiles.
While we were in the park, we got to do something quite interesting. We celebrate September babies with a cake in the wild. Seriously, how fun is it to celebrate your birthday in the wild? I wish I was a September baby. That would have been one to remember.
Before we knew it, it was time to go home.
