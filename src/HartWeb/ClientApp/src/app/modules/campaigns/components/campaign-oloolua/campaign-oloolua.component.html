Oloolua Nature Trails Hike And Fitness!

Welcome and join us for an awesome session of fitness, work out, hike, fun and networking.
Oloolua Nature Trail is a 3.5 kilometer loop trail located near Kajiado, Rift Valley, Kenya that offers the chance to see wildlife and is good for all skill levels. The trail is primarily used for hiking and bird watching. It covers about 661.6 hectares. The trail zigzags and includes many wooden steps and bannisters. It provides an oasis of tranquility for residents looking to escape the city hustle and bustle.

Main Features And Attractions

  • Road trip from Nairobi to Karen in luxurious tour buses with social distancing strictly observed
  • Stop over at the auspicious Karen Water Front to buy snacks in the morning
  • Warm up and stretches
  • Bonding activities with professional facilitators
  • Walking/ jogging round the 5km circuit inside the indigenous forest cover
  • The natural trail intersects with the Mbagathi river that cuts across the forest
  • Marvelous experience with sounds of trickling water and chirping birds in the background
  • Visit to the 37 meters long Mau Mau caves
  • Hiking thru the papyrus swamp
  • Relaxing at the picnic site complete with benches, drop toilets and trash bins where we will also have an opportunity to bond and take our snacks
  • Visit to the 20-foot water fall
  • Hiking with a great team where you can make friends and business networks
