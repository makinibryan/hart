Zip Lining, Archery & Team Building Adventures At Kompass, Ngong Hills

Experience Unique Exhilarating Adrenaline junkies, this one’s for you. Attach yourself to a wire, hold on tight and take the ultimate leap of faith! A breath-taking adrenaline ride of a lifetime, zip lining will leave your heart beating and your hands shaking. A thrilling day out on the zip wires is perfect for the whole squad, so take a browse through what's instore for you.
Your adventure will involve You will be equipped with a bow and arrows and trained on how to use them with precision and safety. You will be shown the path, but it will be up to you, as the protagonist: it’s you who will spot the prey, silently approach it and loose the winning arrow. At the end, after the walking trip, sitting in the wood with a cup of tea and some biscuits, we will celebrate the hunt and your scores.
The outdoor team building activities are designed to enhance team work through fun and interactive team building exercises. The goal of these activities is to increase the participants understanding of team dynamics and to improve how the team works together. Team participation, communication, negotiation and leadership abilities are developed and highlighted in the outdoor team building activities.
