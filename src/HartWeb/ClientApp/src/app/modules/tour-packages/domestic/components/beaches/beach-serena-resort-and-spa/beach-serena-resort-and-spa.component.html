THE SERENA BEACH RESORT & SPA

Overlooking the Indian Ocean to the north of Mombasa town, the Serena Beach Resort & Spa is a gorgeous resort, replicating the winding lanes and colourful market places of a traditional 13th century Swahili town.

SUMMARY

You can dine beneath the stars at the lavishly appointed Fountain Restaurant before enjoying some lively evening entertainment, including floor shows, live music and a disco.
