THE WINSTON HOTEL

Featuring a bar, Grand Winston Hotel is located in Nakuru in the Nakuru County region, 6 km from Westside Mall and 16 km from Lake Nakuru National Park. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel features family rooms.

SUMMARY

There's a restaurant on site. You can enjoy a drink at the bar/lounge. Free breakfast is served daily. WiFi is free in public spaces. The business-friendly Grand Winston Hotel also features complimentary newspapers in the lobby, laundry facilities, and limo/town car service. Self parking is free.
