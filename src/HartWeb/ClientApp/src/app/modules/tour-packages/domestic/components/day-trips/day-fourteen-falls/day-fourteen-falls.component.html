Fourteen Falls

Lake Bogoria is located a distance from Nairobi around 285 Kilometers and we have to start the safari early enough so that we have enough time at the National Reserve. Pick up from Nairobi Hotel latest by 0630 am then depart via the View Point and Nakuru Town to Lake Bogoria National Reserve, you will take at least 4 hours drive from Nairobi.

Fourteen falls are located near Kilimambogo along Thika â€“ Garissa road; you will leave Nairobi either in the morning or afternoon and drive approximately 65 kms via Thika Town. Upon arrival you will meet the local guide for the day activities and guiding.

ACTIVITIES
  • Get information first about the name fourteen falls
  • Short boat ride, the boat guide uses a long pole to guide and move the boat towards the heart of the waterfall
  • Nature walk at the base of the 25 foot water drop
  • Bird watching and photography
  • Swimming/diving by locals

You will spend a couple of Hours later say bye bye to your guide and with your driver drive back to Nairobi. You may stopover at the shopping malls or a restaurant for your Lunch. Upon arrival be transferred to airport or drop off at your Hotel.
