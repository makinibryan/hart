Kicc And Town Center City

The Town center City Tour is available and is scheduled to start from your town hotel or from Mama Ngina street Next to Mama Ngina Java house. Upon a confirmed booking and agreed time our guide will be ready to take you around the city and make sure your explore and discover Nairobi more better. The tour can be joined by a solo traveller or a group of tourist either locals or non residents. Town center city tour will leave you with a great experience that will be shared by our knowledgeable city guides.

You will start with Kenyatta International Convention Center to view Nairobi from all the directions. Walk through the Kenya Parliament and the Nairobi View point among other places with the city center.
