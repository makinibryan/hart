Lake Bogoria National Reserve

Lake Bogoria is located a distance from Nairobi around 285 Kilometers and we have to start the safari early enough so that we have enough time at the National Reserve. Pick up from Nairobi Hotel latest by 0630 am then depart via the View Point and Nakuru Town to Lake Bogoria National Reserve, you will take at least 4 hours drive from Nairobi.

Upon arrival visit the lake to see the Laser Flamingos and the hot water Geysers. Enjoy the warm breeze and if possible the guide shows you where you can bath where the temperatures are not high. After have a game drive on your way out and drive back to Nairobi arriving late in the evening.
