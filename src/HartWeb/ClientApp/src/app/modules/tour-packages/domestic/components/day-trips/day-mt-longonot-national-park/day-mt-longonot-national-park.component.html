Mt Longonot National Park

A day trip to Mt.Longonot is for a pure hiking for at least 5 hrs to 6 hrs from the main gate to crater Rim, Round the crater and returning back to the gate with a professional guide. The day starts in Nairobi with a pick up from the Hotel, drive via the viewpoint to see the escarpment wall, ascent and drive to Longonot Town within a less than an hour.

Your driver will wait you at the gate, after hiking you can drive to Lake Naivasha for Optional activity before returning back to Nairobi where you will be dropped at your Hotel.
