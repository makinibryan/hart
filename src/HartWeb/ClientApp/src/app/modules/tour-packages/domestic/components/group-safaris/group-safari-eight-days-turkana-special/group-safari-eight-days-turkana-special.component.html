DETAILED ITINERARY


Day 1: Nairobi - Baringo

Leaving Nairobi for lake Baringo. Picnic enroute. On arrival at Lake Bogoria in the afternoon, set up camp. Dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Baringo

After breakfast leave Baringo for Maralal. Picnic lunch enroute. A walk after lunch through the most colorful town, before proceeding to Baringo via Maralal, which is on the edge of the Great Rift Valley. Dinner and overnight at south Horr campsite.

Day 3: South Horr - Lake Turkana

After breakfast leave south Horr for Lake Turkana arriving in time for lunch at El Molo campsite, which is on the shore of Lake Turkana. Rest of the day at leisure by the jade sea. Dinner and overnight.


Day 4: Lake Turkana

After breakfast visit the El Molo village. Lunch at campsite. Afternoon at leisure, fishing or swimming if the crocodiles permit. Meals and overnight at the campsite.


Day 5: Turkana - Maralal

After breakfast leave Turkana for Maralal. Lunch enroute, dinner and overnight at Maralal.


Day 6: Maralal - Samburu

After breakfast leave Maralal for Samburu game reserve. On arrival proceed to the campsite and thereafter proceed for a late evening game drive. Return to the campsite for dinner and overnight.


Day 7: Samburu

Early morning pre breakfast game drive. Return to the camp for breakfast, 1000 hrs. After breakfast further game drive. Lunch 1600 hrs, after lunch a third game drive. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.


Day 8: Samburu - Mt Kenya - Nairobi

After breakfast leave Samburu for Naro Moru river lodge. Picnic lunch enroute. Drop off clients who want to remain behind for climbing Mt Kenya. After lunch leave for Nairobi.
