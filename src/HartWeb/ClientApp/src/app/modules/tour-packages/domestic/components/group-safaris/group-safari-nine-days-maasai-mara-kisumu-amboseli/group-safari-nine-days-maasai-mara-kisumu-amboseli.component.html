Maasai Mara, Kisumu, Bondo/Kogelo village, Nakuru and Amboseli.

0930 hrs. Depart Nairobi for Maasai Mara Game reserve. Picnic lunch enroute. On arrival at the game reserve, proceed for a game drive enroute to camp/lodge search for black manned lions, elephants, leopards, cheetah, buffalo and other animals. Proceed to the camp/lodge for dinner and overnight.

DETAILED ITINERARY


Day 1: Nairobi - Maasai Mara

Leaving Nairobi for lake Baringo. Picnic enroute. On arrival at Lake Bogoria in the afternoon, set up camp. Dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Maasai Mara

Full day is spent exploring this unique park, which forms the northern part of the famous Serengeti National Park. Meals and overnight at the camp/lodge.

Day 3: Maasai Mara

Full day game viewing with morning and evening game drives. Afternoon will be spent at leisure with optional activities. All meals and overnight at camp/lodge.


Day 4: Maasai Mara - Kisumu

Morning game drives in Maasai Mara. After breakfast drive to Kisumu/Bondo for overnight.


Day 5: Kisumu/Bondo - Kogelo village.

A full day spent exploring this village. This is the ancestral home of the first Black American President - Barrack Obama. Overnight at Kisumu/Bondo hotel.


Day 6: Kisumu - Nakuru

After breakfast drive to Nakuru for overnight at a budget Hotel/lodge.


Day 7: Nakuru - Nairobi - Amboseli

After breakfast proceed to Nakuru National park for a game drive, leave Nakuru for Nairobi and proceed to Amboseli. Lunch is served enroute. Once in the national park a game viewing enroute to the camp base. Dinner and overnight at the lodge/camp.


Day 8: Amboseli

Full in Amboseli (am and pm game drive) with a chance to see rhinos, lion, giraffe, herds of elephants and many other animals with Mt Kilimanjaro as the backdrop. Dinner and overnight at the camp/lodge.


Day 9: Amboseli – Nairobi

After breakfast leave the lodge for the last game viewing as you leave Amboseli for Nairobi in the late afternoon.
