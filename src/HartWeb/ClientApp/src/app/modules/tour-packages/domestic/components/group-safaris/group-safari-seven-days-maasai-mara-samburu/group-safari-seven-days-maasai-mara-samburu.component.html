Maasai Mara, Lake Nakuru and Samburu

Want to have the experience of a life-time and enjoy the Majesty of African Safari, Climb Mount Kenya or lay down in the white sands of Mombasa? Just drop us a message with what you have in mind. We will strive get back to you ASAP.


Option 5th Day: Lake Nakuru or Amboseli.

DETAILED ITINERARY


Day 1: Nairobi- Maasai Mara

Depart Nairobi for Maasai Mara. Picnic lunch enroute. On arrival at the game reserve, proceed for a game drive enroute the camp in search of black manned lions, elephants, cheetah, buffalo and other plains game. Proceed to the camp for dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Maasai Mara

Full day is spent exploring this unique park, which forms the northern parts of the famous Serengeti National Park. Meals and overnight at the base camp.

Day 3: Maasai Mara

Full day game viewing is provided. Meals and overnight at the camp/lodge.


Day 4: Maasai Mara - Nakuru

After breakfast leave camp for Lake Nakuru. Picnic enroute at the safari junction. After lunch proceed to lake Elementaita stopping for panoramic and scenery viewing. You will arrive in Nakuru later in the afternoon, dinner and overnight in your hotel or camp.


Day 5: Option 1 - Samburu

Depart Nakuru after breakfast and drive north via Mt Kenya. Picnic enroute. Proceed to Samburu game reserve in time for a short game drive enroute to the camp. Dinner and overnight at the camp.


Day 6: Samburu

Full day game viewing in Samburu in search for the ORYX GERENUK, gravy zebra, reticulated giraffe, crocodile and hippos along the ewaso nyiro river. Meals and overnight at the camp.


Day 7: Samburu - Nairobi

0630 hrs. Pre-breakfast game drive. After breakfast return to Nairobi with a picnic lunch arriving late afternoon.


Day 5: Option 2 - Nakuru - Nairobi - Amboseli

After breakfast leave Nakuru for Nairobi and proceed to Amboseli. Lunch is served enroute. Once in the National park a game viewing enroute to the camp. Dinner and Overnight at the Camp.


Day 6: Option 3 - Amboseli

After breakfast leave camp for the last game viewing as you leave Amboseli for Nairobi. Picnic lunch enroute. Arrive in Nairobi in the afternoon.
