DETAILED ITINERARY


Day 1: Nairobi- Maasai Mara

0930 hrs. Depart from our Nairobi Office for a journey south along the floor of the rift valley. Lunch break at Narok and then climb western wall entering the Hemmingway’s county Serengeti Plains. Arrive late afternoon in the Maasai Mara game reserve, game drive enroute. Dinner and overnight at the campsite/lodge.

Day 2: Maasai Mara

Full day in search of elephants, zebra, wildebeest, rhino, buffalo, lion, cheetah, leopard, and other plains game. All meals overnight at the base camp lodge.

Day 3: Maasai Mara

Full day game viewing. Meals and overnight at the camp lodge.


Day 4: Maasai Mara - Nakuru

After breakfast leave camp for Lake Nakuru. Picnic enroute at the safari junction. After lunch proceed to lake Elementaita stopping for panoramic and scenery viewing. You will arrive in Nakuru later in the afternoon, dinner and overnight in your hotel or camp.


Day 5: Nakuru - Lake Bogoria and Baringo

After an early breakfast, drive to Lake Baringo via Lake Bogoria. With picnic Lunch, explore on foot these naturalist lakes and view the flamingo in the thousands. Caution must be taken while walking near the hot springs as they are super-hot. Return to Nakuru for dinner and overnight in your hotel or camp.


Day 6: Lake Nakuru Park – Nairobi

0630 hrs. Game drive in Lake Nakuru Park, which is renowned for its large variety of birds. Besides bird for which its famous, it is possible to see other game such as waterbucks, impala, buffalo and gazelle in the surrounding gardens. Return to your hotel for breakfast. After breakfast return to Nairobi arriving in the afternoon.
