12 Days Bush and Beach Safari

Start your Kenyan family safari in Nairobi; the vibrant, exciting place and Africa’s fourth largest city. You and your family/friends will explore Nairobi's cafe culture, unbridled nightlife, National Museum, the Karen Blixen Museum.

DETAILED ITINERARY

Day 1-2: Welcome to Kenya

On arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, you will be met and transferred to the Hotel. This will be your home for the next two nights with your bed and breakfast included.
Enjoy buffet dinner as well as city tour that will give you the opportunity to spot Nairobi's National Museum, the Karen Blixen Museum, unbridled nightlife and cafe culture.

Day 3-4: Lake Nakuru National Park

Drive to Lake Naivasha Sopa Lodge; set in 150 acres of grasslands studded with Acacia bushes and trees. The resort is not only home to resident giraffe, waterbuck and both Vervet and Colobus monkeys, but it is also a night stop for the hippos when they leave the lake every night to come and trim the grass on the expansive lawns.
You will stay for two nights with all your meals included. Highlights will be boat rides on the lake, morning and afternoon game drives, picnic lunch and nature walks.

Day 5-7: Kings of the Jungle Tour

Masai Mara Sopa Lodge is one of the first lodges to be built in the Masai Mara Game Reserve. It's located on the slopes of Oloongams Hills. This will be your abode for the next three nights with all your meals included.

Day 8-11: Beach Excursion

Drive to Nairobi and after lunch, you will be transferred to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for the scheduled flight to Mombasa. When you arrive at Mombasa Airport you will be transported to the a prestigous hotel Beach Resort & Spa.
Spend four nights with your bed and breakfast as well as lunch included (Half Board). Enjoy the beauty of Mombasa and visit the Minazi Café, Lido Seafood Grill, Cocos Beach Bar, Tulia Spa & Health Bar and over the Indian ocean.

Day 12: End of Service

After breakfast, you and your family will be transferred to Mombasa airport for your scheduled flight to Nairobi.
