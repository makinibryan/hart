Maasai Mara, Lake Nakuru

Want to have the experience of a life-time and enjoy the Majesty of African Safari, Climb Mount Kenya or lay down in the white sands of Mombasa? Just drop us a message with what you have in mind. We will strive get back to you ASAP.

DETAILED ITINERARY


Day 1: Nairobi- Maasai Mara

0930 hrs. Leave Nairobi for Maasai Mara Game Reserve. Picnic lunch enroute. On arrival at the game reserve, proceed for a game drive enroute to the camp in the search of black manned lions, elephants, leopards, cheetah, buffalo and other plains game. Proceed to the camp for dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Maasai Mara

Full day is spent exploring this unique park, which really forms the northern part of the Serengeti National Park. Meals and overnight at the camp.

Day 3: Maasai Mara

Full day game viewing is provided. Meals and overnight stay at the camp lodge.


Day 4: Maasai Mara - Nakuru

After breakfast leave the camp for Nakuru the world renowned for its masses of flamingo and its often referred to as the pink Lake. Besides bird it is possible to see small game like waterbuck, impala, rhino and buffalo in the garden surrounding. Dinner and overnight at the hotel.


Day 5: Nakuru - Nairobi

After breakfast proceed for a game drive and depart for Nairobi arriving in the afternoon.
