Hemmingway Trail, Maasai Mara

Want to have the experience of a life-time and enjoy the Majesty of African Safari, Climb Mount Kenya or lay down in the white sands of Mombasa? Just drop us a message with what you have in mind. We will strive get back to you ASAP.

DETAILED ITINERARY


Day 1: Nairobi- Maasai Mara

0900 hrs. Depart from our Nairobi office for a journey south along the flour of the rift valley. Lunch break at Narok and then climb western wall entering the Hemmingway’s county Serengeti Plains. Arrive late afternoon in the Maasai Mara game reserve, game drive enroute. Dinner and overnight at the campsite/lodge.

Day 2: Maasai Mara

Whole day in Mara. Morning and afternoon game drives. Meals and overnight at the campsite lodge.

Day 3: Maasai Mara - Nairobi

After breakfast, depart for Nairobi. Enjoy picnic lunch enroute and arrive in Nairobi evening 1800 hrs.
