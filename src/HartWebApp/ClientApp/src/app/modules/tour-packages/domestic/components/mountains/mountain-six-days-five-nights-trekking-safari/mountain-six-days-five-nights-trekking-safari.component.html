6 Days Chogoria Easy Pace

Want to have the experience of a life-time and enjoy the Majesty of African Safari, Climb Mount Kenya or lay down in the white sands of Mombasa? Just drop us a message with what you have in mind. We will strive get back to you ASAP.

DETAILED ITINERARY


Day:1
Depart from Nairobi at 0900 hrs to Nanyuki for dinner and overnight.

Day:2
After breakfast vehicle transfers to Sirimon Route, hike to Old Moses camp (3300) for overnight 6 to 8hrs hike.

Day 3:
Hike to Shipton’s camp (42000m) for overnight 5 to 7hrs hike.

Day 4:
Attempt Lenana peak (4985m) at 0300 hrs and descend to Minto’s hut for overnight.

Day 5:
Hike leisurely to Chogoria Park Gate (3020m) for overnight.

Day 6:
Descend leisurely to Chogoria town for transfer back to Nairobi.
