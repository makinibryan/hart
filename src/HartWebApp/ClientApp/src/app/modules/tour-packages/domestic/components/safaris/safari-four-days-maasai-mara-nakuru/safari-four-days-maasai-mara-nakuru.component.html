Maasai Mara, Nakuru

Want to have the experience of a life-time and enjoy the Majesty of African Safari, Climb Mount Kenya or lay down in the white sands of Mombasa? Just drop us a message with what you have in mind. We will strive get back to you ASAP.

DETAILED ITINERARY


Day 1: Nairobi- Maasai Mara

0930 hrs. Leave Nairobi for Maasai Mara game reserve. Picnic lunch enroute. On arrival at the game reserve, proceed for a game drive enroute to the camp in the search of black manned lion, leapord and cheetah.

Day 2: Maasai Mara

Full day is spent exploring this unique park, which really forms the northern part of the Serengeti National Park. Meals and overnight at the camp.

Day 3: Maasai Mara - Nakuru

Morning game drives in Maasai Mara. Leave Maasai Mara and proceed to Lake Nakuru. Dinner and overnight in a budget hotel.


Day 4: Nakuru - Nairobi

After breakfast proceed for a game drive and depart for Nairobi arriving in the afternoon.
