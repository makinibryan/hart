3 Days Maasai Mara Tented Entumoto Camp

Want to have the experience of a life-time and enjoy the Majesty of African Safari, Climb Mount Kenya or lay down in the white sands of Mombasa? Just drop us a message with what you have in mind. We will strive get back to you ASAP.

DETAILED ITINERARY


DAY 1: NAIROBI – MASAI MARA

Pick up from Airport or Nairobi Hotel by 0700am, be introduced to the driver guide and departure for Masai Mara. Make a Stopover at the Great Rift Valley Escarpment wall, descend the wall and drive to Narok Town and further to Sekenani Gate. Proceed to Entumoto Safari Camp arriving in Time for hot lunch, check in followed by little siesta at your tent. You will have evening game drive at the conservancy and sundowner returning to the camp for Dinner and overnight.

DAY 2: FULL DAY GAME DRIVE

Early breakfast at the camp, then with your guide leave with picnic lunch for a full day game drive at Maasai Mara game reserve. Tracking the animal’s upto Mara River or Talek River to see the Hippopotamus and crocodiles, Picnic lunch at the picnic site then continue with the game drive till 1700hrs, return to the camp for optional activity, you will decide either to visit the maasai village or have another Sundowner, return to the camp for dinner and overnight.

DAY 3: MASAI MARA – NAIROBI

Early morning with Absolute Holiday Safari guide have a morning game drive with the conservancy or Masai Mara game reserve, return to the camp for full breakfast, check out and driver back to Nairobi where you will be dropped at your Nairobi Hotel or be transferred to direct to airport to catch a flight to other Destination.
