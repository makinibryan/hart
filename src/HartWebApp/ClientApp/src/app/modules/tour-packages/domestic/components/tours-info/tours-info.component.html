Mountain Tours

Mt Kenya is the second highest mountain Africa and it is one of the most beautiful. Its ecosystem supports a vast array of animals and people within its region of eastern and central Kenya. The mountain stands tall at 5,199 meters and provides water for over 50% of Kenyans with its mineral-rich water sources. The sheer size of the mountain makes it a delightful challenge to tackle with different points of scaling it. It also offers diverse climates and glorious views in those different scaling points that up to the level of difficulty or lowers it.

We as H-ART OF AFRICA SAFARIS LTD offer the best and most competitive packages in the market. We introduce our clients to little known and magnificent routes like the Kirinyaga route that is covered with a beautiful forest, bamboo, and shrubs. But we also offer packages with the other routes and their advantages too. H-ART OF AFRICA SAFARIS LTD ensures you are very comfortable on the trek and we also assist you by recommending the best gear for scaling the mountain. We also customize our packages to suit you our client.

Kirinyaga Route

The trek begins from the prestigious castle lodge where the current Queen of England stayed in her visit to Kenya. It is 6 days long walk up the mountain covered with amazing vegetation and wildlife. The landscape is unrivalled and the route is very beautiful.

Chogoria Rout

It is in the neighboring county from the Kirinyaga route and it is considered to be very scenic and rewarding due to its challenging climb. It takes 5 to 6 days.

Naro Moru Route

It is the shortest and close to the top, is covered with stunning glacial valleys. H-ART OF AFRICA SAFARIS LTD offers a package of 4 to 5 days.

Sirimon Route

This route is less crowded and it is another that is rarely used but, offers magnificent scenes dotted with stunning wildlife. It takes 5 to 6 days.
